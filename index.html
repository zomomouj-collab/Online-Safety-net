About SafeNet

SafeNet is an educational platform designed specifically for MYP (Middle Years Programme) students to learn about online safety. My goal is to empower young people with the knowledge and skills needed to navigate the digital world confidently and securely.

Created by Janet Zomomou, a dedicated student passionate about online safety education, SafeNet provides interactive content covering stranger safety, scam awareness, social media privacy, and more. Through engaging quizzes and comprehensive guides, students can develop critical thinking skills to identify and avoid online threats.
